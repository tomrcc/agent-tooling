# Astrofy — Editor Guide

Welcome to your portfolio site! This guide explains how to manage your content.

## Quick links

- [Pages](cloudcannon:collections/pages) — Homepage, Projects, Services, CV
- [Blog posts](cloudcannon:collections/blog) — Your blog articles
- [Store](cloudcannon:collections/store) — Products and digital items

## Pages

Your site has several editable pages. Open any page from the **Pages** collection in the sidebar to edit it.

- **Homepage** — Your landing page with hero section, featured projects, and latest blog posts
- **Projects** — Showcase your work with cards organized into sections
- **Services** — List the services you offer
- **CV** — Your resume with education, experience, certifications, and skills

You can create new pages using the **+ Add** button in the Pages collection. Two types are available:

- **Page** — A simple page with a title and text content
- **Card Listing** — A page with sections of project/service cards

The Homepage and CV are one-of-a-kind pages — edit the existing ones rather than creating new copies.

## Blog

Write and manage blog posts from the **Blog** collection. Each post has a title, description, publication date, optional hero image, badge, and tags.

To create a new post, click **+ Add** in the Blog collection. Posts are sorted by publication date, newest first.

## Store

Manage your products and digital items from the **Store** collection. Each item has a title, description, pricing, and optional checkout link.

## Editing tips

- **Visual editor** — Click directly on text and images on the page to edit them inline
- **Content editor** — Use the markdown editor for writing blog posts and long-form content
- **Data editor** — Use the sidebar panel to edit structured fields like dates, tags, and settings
- **Images** — Click any image in the visual editor to replace it using the image picker
- **Arrays** — Items like project cards, timeline entries, and skills can be added, removed, and reordered using the controls that appear when you hover over them
