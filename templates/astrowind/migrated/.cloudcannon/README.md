# Welcome to AstroWind

This is a multi-purpose website with several types of pages, a blog, and flexible page building. You can manage all of your content directly from this CMS.

## Pages

General site pages live in the **Pages** section of the sidebar. These include pages like About, Contact, Pricing, Services, Terms, and Privacy.

**Creating a page:** Click **+ Add** and choose a type:
- **Page** -- a standard page with a title, description, and body content
- **Page Builder** -- a flexible page assembled from reusable content blocks

**Editing a page:** Click any page to open it. Use the **Visual** editor to see a live preview, or switch to the **Data** view to edit page details as a form.

**Deleting a page:** Open the page, then use the menu (three dots) in the top right.

**Page builder pages:** When editing a page builder page, scroll down to the "Content Blocks" section to add, remove, or reorder blocks. Each block type has its own fields -- click a block to expand and edit it.

## Home pages

Home page variants live in the **Pages > Homes** section. These are alternative homepage designs (SaaS, Startup, Mobile App, Personal) that showcase different layouts and content.

Editing works the same as regular pages. Each home page has its own set of sections and content blocks.

## Landing pages

Marketing and campaign pages live in the **Pages > Landing** section. These include lead generation, sales, product detail, and pre-launch pages.

Creating, editing, and deleting landing pages works the same as regular pages.

## Blog posts

Blog posts live in the **Blogging > Post** section of the sidebar.

**Editing a post:** Click any post to open it. You can use:
- **Content** -- focused writing view with formatting toolbar
- **Data** -- edit post details as a form

**Post fields to know about:**
- **Title** and **Description** -- the headline and summary for listings and search results
- **Image** -- the post's featured image
- **Category** -- a single category for the post
- **Tags** -- additional labels for organizing posts
- **Draft** -- toggle on to hide the post from the live site
- **Publish Date** -- controls when the post appears

## Site settings

Configuration files live in the **Site Settings** section of the sidebar:

- **Navigation** -- control the header menu links (including dropdowns), header action buttons, footer link groups, social media links, and the footer note. This is where you manage what visitors see in the site's navigation and footer.
- **Config** -- general site settings including the site name, URL, SEO metadata (page title template, description, social sharing images), blog settings (posts per page, URL structure), analytics, and theme (light/dark mode).
