# Welcome to AstroPaper

This is a blog-focused website. You can manage your blog posts and pages directly from this CMS.

## Quick links

- [Blog posts](cloudcannon:collections/blog)
- [Pages](cloudcannon:collections/pages)

## Blog posts

Your blog posts live in the **Content > Blog** section of the sidebar.

**Creating a post:** Click the **+ Add** button at the top of the Blog collection and choose "Blog Post". Fill in a title, description, author, and publication date, then start writing your content.

**Editing a post:** Click any post to open it. You can edit using three different views:
- **Visual** -- see a live preview of your post as you edit
- **Content** -- a focused writing view with a formatting toolbar
- **Data** -- edit the post's details (title, tags, date, etc.) as a form

**Deleting a post:** Open the post, then use the menu (three dots) in the top right to delete it.

**Post fields to know about:**
- **Title** -- the post's headline
- **Description** -- a short summary, shown in listings and search results
- **Slug** -- controls the URL of the post (e.g. a slug of `my-first-post` gives the URL `/posts/my-first-post/`)
- **Tags** -- categorize your post; you can pick existing tags or create new ones
- **Featured** -- toggle this on to highlight the post on the homepage
- **Draft** -- toggle this on to hide the post from the live site
- **OG Image** -- an optional image used when the post is shared on social media

## Pages

Your site pages live in the **Pages** section of the sidebar. These include the homepage and any other standalone pages.

Pages are edited using the **Visual** editor, which shows a live preview of the page as you make changes. Click on text or images directly in the preview to edit them.

New pages cannot be created from the CMS because each page requires a matching template. Contact a developer if you need a new page added.

To delete a page, open it and use the menu (three dots) in the top right. Be careful -- deleting a page removes it from the site entirely.
