# Welcome to Astroship

This is a business website with a blog and team profiles. You can manage your pages, blog posts, and team members directly from this CMS.

## Quick links

- [Pages](cloudcannon:collections/pages)
- [Blog posts](cloudcannon:collections/blog)
- [Team members](cloudcannon:collections/team)

## Pages

Your site pages live in the **Pages** section of the sidebar. These include the homepage, about page, pricing page, and contact page.

**Editing a page:** Click any page to open it. Use the **Visual** editor to see a live preview as you make changes, or switch to the **Data** view to edit page details as a form.

Each page has its own layout and fields. For example, the homepage has a banner, features list, and call-to-action section, while the pricing page has a list of pricing plans.

**Adding or deleting pages:** This site uses fixed page layouts, so new pages cannot be created from the CMS. Each page has a dedicated design that needs developer involvement to set up. Contact a developer if you need a new page. To delete a page, open it and use the menu (three dots) in the top right -- but be careful, as restoring a deleted page requires developer help.

## Blog posts

Your blog posts live in the **Blogging > Blog** section of the sidebar.

**Creating a post:** Click **+ Add** and choose "Blog Post". Fill in a title, description, author, and publication date, then write your content.

**Editing a post:** Click any post to open it. You can use:
- **Content** -- focused writing view with formatting toolbar
- **Visual** -- live preview of your post
- **Data** -- edit post details as a form

**Deleting a post:** Open the post, then use the menu (three dots) in the top right.

**Post fields to know about:**
- **Image** -- the post's featured image (with alt text for accessibility)
- **Category** -- a single category for the post
- **Tags** -- additional labels; you can pick existing tags or create new ones
- **Draft** -- toggle on to hide the post from the live site

When writing posts, you can insert a **Button** component using the **+** button in the formatting toolbar.

## Team members

Team profiles live in the **Blogging > Team** section of the sidebar.

**Creating a team member:** Click **+ Add** and choose "Team Member". Fill in their name, title, and avatar image.

**Editing a team member:** Click a team member to open their profile. Team members are displayed on the about page.

**Deleting a team member:** Open the profile and use the menu (three dots) in the top right.
